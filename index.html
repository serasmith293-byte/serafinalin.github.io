Materials
FRP vs Carbon Fiber
We help you choose the right material for your application and budget.

🔶 FRP (Fiberglass)

  • Cost-effective for medium to large parts
  • Excellent corrosion and weather resistance
  • RF transparent — ideal for radomes
  • Good strength-to-weight ratio
  • Flame retardant grades available
  • Wide color and surface finish options

⬛ Carbon Fiber

  • Highest strength-to-weight ratio
  • Premium aesthetic — visible weave finish
  • Excellent stiffness and fatigue resistance
  • Ideal for aerospace, UAV, motorsport
  • EMI shielding capability
  • Prepreg & wet layup options
